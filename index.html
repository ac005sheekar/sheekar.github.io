About Me

I am a prospective graduate student of Computer Science. Currently, I am working as a Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IUBAT, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I completed my BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from IUBAT in 2020. Between 2021 to 2024, I worked as a Researcher and Deep Learning Algorithm Developer in Cisscom (United States), KaleidoSoft (Croatia) and Vinacts (South Korea).

I have collaborated with core engineers in Hyundai and Volvo 360c Self-Driving Car projects and in Samsung for computer vision algorithms for S24 Ultra release features.

In 2025, during my days in the academia, I remotely worked on a complex 4D CTP data enabled Medical Image Synthesis project under the supervision of Dr. Mohammad Arafat Hussain, who is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Harvard Medical School.
