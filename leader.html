I led my research team and represented my undergrad University (IUBAT) at 10 National Technological Competitions, Hackathons and Expo. I ended up winning Runner Up Titles at 3 National Tech Competitions in 2019 and 2020.

Runner UP at Mujib Borsho IT Carnival 2020, Dhaka International University



I led my research team "SENSO CODER" from IUBAT CSE Robotics Club at Mujib Borsho IT Carnival 2020 Organized by Dhaka International University (DIU). We secured our position of runner-up at the category of Tech Idea Contest. We demonstrated the idea of "The Smart Injector" over there. The other team member was Saleh Bin Sayeem. It was a National Contest where 17 nation-wide selected teams got the chance to participate and only 5 teams could make it to the final round.


First Runners UP at JU CSE Fest 2019, Jahangirnagar University



Under my leadership, "SENSO CODER" also achieved First Runners Up position amongst the top 11 nationwide selected teams at the Tech Idea Competition of JU CSE FEST 2019 powered by Tiger IT at Jahangirnagar University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Here we presented our second phase implementation of the World’s first ever Fully Automated Multiple Injection System with Real Time Clock (RTC) feature. The team members were Saleh Bin Sayeem, Mohammad Ali Shuvo and Nafi Shariar.


Second Runner UP at Innovation Competition 2019, IUBAT



My leadership also garnered "SENSO CODER" to achieve Second Runners Up position amongst the top 21 selected projects at the Innovation Competition 2019 powered by iLab innovation, Bangladesh ICT Ministry, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Here we presented our first phase implementation of the World’s first ever Fully Automated Multiple Injection System with Real Time Clock (RTC) feature. The team members were Saleh Bin Sayeem, Mohammad Ali Shuvo and Nafi Shariar.


MindSparks 2020, Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST)



We demonstrated Environment Monitoring System with Machine Learning based Predictive Features at MindSparks'20, AUST. This time Saleh Bin Sayeem led our team "SENSO CODER" and I was the second in command. We developed the complex circuitry of monitoring platform on Arduino ATMega and Sensors (Sound, DHT11 temperature-humidity, dust, CO2 and YL69 pH). I implemented a library with The Random Forest Algorithm to get the predictive features of data analysis within the platfrom. The other team members were Kamrul and Tawfiq Anam.

I represented my Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IUBAT at the International Smart City Expo'19 where I demonstrated the Sensor based Multiple Injection Prototype with Real Time Clock (RTC) Feature in front of Mr. Atiqul Islam, The Honorable Mayor of Dhaka-North City Corporation (The image on the left). Mecceleration 2019, Islamic University of Technology (IUT)



I led "SENSO CODER" in the National Project Showcasing Competition called "Mecceleration'19" at IUT. We demonstrated "The Smart Injector" for the very first time. Robo Carnival 2019, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)



I led my robotics team to participate in a National Robotics Competition at BUET called Robo Carnival 2019. We fought at the Segment of "Robo Soccer" but got eliminated in the quarter final round. The other team members were Rajon Sarkar, Mohammad Ali Shuvo and Tarikol Islam Touhid. After failing miserably, I shifted another research idea from this robotic prototype which came up with the manifestation of "Nano Rover". This "Nano Rover" later on had its own identity as a multi-sensory surveillance robot with modified inception-net. This research ended winning a "Best Paper Award" in a Springer Nature affiliated international conference in 2021.

Smart Hackathon 2018, Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP)



It was time to participate in a National Hackathon to solve some real world problems. We joined BUP ICT Fest Hackathon 2018 where we developed an efficient online examination taking platform so that students can avoid the complexities coming from that physical participation. We achieved sixth position in this Competition. The other team members were Mohammad Ali Shuvo and Redwan Ahmed Khan.


ROBOLUTION 2018, Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST)



This was the first time I was participating in any national competition. I chose the countries biggest robotics event ROBOLUTION'18 to participate in. I assembled my team "SENSO CODER" and started developing our autonomous line following robot. Despite having no prior experience, we still secured our place at the finals over the event. We achieved eleventh place after fighting against 75 other teams from the entire country. The other team members were Mohammad Gulzar Hossain, Rajon Sarkar and Hasibul Islam Polok.
